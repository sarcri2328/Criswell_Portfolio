Summary
All of the current framing software on the market is too expensive, or too complicated for the average framer to use. An app that can calculate frame prices and sizes accurately and quickly was needed, so I set out to learn how to make one. I figured even if I was the only one who ever used it, I could add value to the business and help bring in more money in less time. Fast forward almost 2 years later after the intial deployment, it has saved countless hours of calculation work and selling frames to customers, while reducing the cost of incorrect measurements and underselling our services.
